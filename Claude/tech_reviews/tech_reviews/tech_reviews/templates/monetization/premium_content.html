NVIDIA RTX 5090 Early Benchmarks

Posted: September 5, 2024

Exclusive performance testing of NVIDIA's upcoming flagship GPU based on the Blackwell architecture.

Our engineering sample of the RTX 5090 shows a massive 65% performance uplift over the RTX 4090 in rasterization performance, with even greater gains in ray tracing workloads. The new architecture features 50% more CUDA cores and a significantly improved AI tensor core design.

Memory bandwidth has been increased to 1.5TB/s thanks to the new GDDR7 memory running at 32Gbps. Power consumption remains similar to the 4090 at 450W, but with much better thermal performance due to the new vapor chamber cooling solution.

Game Performance (4K Ultra)
  • Cyberpunk 2077: 145 FPS (with Ray Reconstruction)
  • Microsoft Flight Simulator: 112 FPS
  • Alan Wake 2: 98 FPS (with Path Tracing)
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III: 187 FPS

Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra Camera Analysis

Posted: August 28, 2024

Deep dive into Samsung's next camera system with sample images and video analysis.

Our early testing of the Galaxy S25 Ultra's camera system reveals a completely new 250MP sensor with larger 2.0μm pixels after binning. The new periscope telephoto now offers 15x optical zoom with improved optical image stabilization.

Low-light performance has seen significant improvements, with noise reduction algorithms that preserve more detail while eliminating grain. Video recording now supports 8K at 60fps with improved stabilization and HDR10+ support.

Camera Specifications
  • Main: 250MP ISOCELL HP3, f/1.7, OIS
  • Ultra-wide: 48MP, f/2.2, 120° FOV
  • Telephoto 1: 50MP, 3x optical, f/2.4
  • Telephoto 2: 50MP, 10x optical, f/3.5
  • Front: 40MP, f/2.2, AF with PDAF
